Run abSENSE on your own data

Use this page to run an analysis in a taxon or gene not included in our precomputed database.

You'll need the N evolutionary distances between your focal species and each of your N other species.

You'll also need the bitscore of at least 2 orthologs of your gene of interest (in the focal species) in 2 of the N other species. (You'll also need the bitscore of the gene against itself in the focal species.)
